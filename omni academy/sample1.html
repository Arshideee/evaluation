Welcome to Omni Academies, a community of learners, thinkers, creatives, and entrepreneurs in a brand new alternative secondary school in Toowong, Brisbane.

Our approach is purposeful. Educators guide students in their growth and learning, facilitating all facets of their knowledge and providing space to define their role in an ever-changing world.

Omni is a holistic educational provider, engaging students, families, and the broader community; united through academic, cultural and future-focused pursuits.

What makes Omni different?

Omni students are creative, aspirational, motivated, digitally fluent and empowered risk-takers and innovators

creative, aspirational, motivated, digitally fluent and empowered risk-takers and innovators At Omni Academies, students are invited to be autonomous in their thinking and learning; to have agency over the ways in which they learn. This approach allows for students to be intrinsically motivated, possessing the self-determination required for success in all endeavours.


Omni incorporates student voice; encouraging young people to develop the skills and confidence to advocate for themselves and others as active participants in their education. This results in deeper learning experiences, and a highly developed understanding of who they are in their world.
